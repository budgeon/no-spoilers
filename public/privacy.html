Privacy Policy · Last updated July 2026

What we collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • A profile avatar (emoji you choose from a preset list)

When you use the app, we store:

  • TV shows, movies, and episodes you mark as watched
  • Ratings you assign to titles
  • Your watchlist
  • Friends you follow and who follows you
  • Activity timestamps (when you watched something)

How we use your data

We use your data solely to provide the NoSpoilers service — to track your watch history, show your stats, and share activity with friends you choose to follow. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

Third-party services

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider. Your data is stored on Supabase's infrastructure. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • TMDB — we use The Movie Database API to fetch show and movie metadata. No personal data is sent to TMDB.
  • Google OAuth — if you sign in with Google, your name and email are shared with us by Google. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

Your rights

You can update your profile name and avatar at any time from the app. To request a full copy or deletion of your data, contact us at the email below.
